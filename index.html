About

Seller Flow is a unified SaaS platform for Amazon sellers.

Seller Flow empowers Amazon sellers to streamline every aspect of their business—from real-time inventory management and order processing to profit analytics and customer messaging—within a single, intuitive dashboard. By automating routine tasks and centralizing critical data, Seller Flow helps small teams focus on growth and maximize profitability. The platform is under continuous development, with regular updates and new features planned to further empower Amazon sellers on their journey to success.

Features

Inventory Management

With Seller Flow’s Inventory Management, you gain real-time visibility into stock levels across all your Amazon marketplaces. Automatic SKU merging intelligently consolidates duplicate listings, preventing oversells and reducing manual reconciliation. Set custom low-stock alerts and reorder thresholds to ensure you never run out of your top-selling items. Predictive restocking suggestions use historical sales data to help you optimize replenishment timing and avoid costly stockouts.

Order Synchronization

Our Order Synchronization feature centralizes every incoming order into a single dashboard, no matter how many marketplaces or regions you sell in. Orders are fetched in real-time via the SP-API, with status updates (pending, unshipped, shipped) automatically reflected in Seller Flow. Built-in shipment tracking integration keeps your customers informed and minimizes support inquiries. You can also batch-process order confirmations and cancellations to streamline your fulfillment workflows.

Profit Analytics

Profit Analytics delivers a comprehensive financial overview by combining product costs, Amazon fees, shipping expenses, and other overheads. Interactive dashboards break down your net margins by SKU, marketplace, and time period, so you always know which products drive profitability. Customizable reports and export options make it easy to share insights with your team or investors. Armed with granular data, you can make informed pricing and inventory decisions that maximize your bottom line.

Customer Messaging

Seller Flow’s Customer Messaging unifies all buyer communications—emails, Amazon Buyer-Seller messages, and return inquiries—into one intuitive inbox. Smart templates and canned responses help you reply faster while maintaining a personal touch. Automated tagging and SLA tracking ensure no message falls through the cracks, improving your response times and seller rating. All conversations are logged and searchable, giving you full context to resolve issues and build customer loyalty.
